How it works

Profile

Here, all of your projects will be stored in one place. Designing is a process which means your space will evolve over time. Come back to your designs when you're ready to freshen up your look again.

Upload

Upload pictures of existing furniture, specify the dimensions of your room and set your budget. Knowing what you are working with will help you to make informed decisions adding elements to your room.

Visualize

Create a vision for your room. Browse through photos and furniture, and pick out pieces to add and move around.

Collaborate

Don’t design alone! Invite roommates, family members or a significant other to design with you. Everyone will have workspace access to easily communicate ideas and bring your visions together.

Consult

Feeling stuck? Browse our collection of designer profiles. Find the one you connect with and request help at any stage of your project.

Buy

Finally, it’s time to bring your room home! The last step is the easiest, click on furniture to purchase.
